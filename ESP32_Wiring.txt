          Smart Cat Box - Project Hardware Wiring Map
          Date: 2026-03-28 | Controller: ESP32-S3

[Part 1: Control & Logic Link (Low Power)]
------------------------------------------------------------
From (Component)    | Pin/Port       | To (Target)      | Function
------------------------------------------------------------
MacBook Pro M4      | USB-C Port     | ESP32-S3 USB-C   | Power & Flash
ESP32-S3            | 5V (VCC)       | Relay VCC (DC+)  | Relay Logic Power
ESP32-S3            | GND            | Relay GND (DC-)  | Common Ground
ESP32-S3            | D2 (GPIO 2)    | Relay IN (SIG)   | Control Signal
------------------------------------------------------------

[Part 2: Power & Heating Link (High Power - Isolated)]
------------------------------------------------------------
From (Source)       | Connection     | To (Load)        | Function
------------------------------------------------------------
Power Bank (5V)     | Positive (+)   | Relay COM        | Main Power Input
Relay               | NO (Normal Open)| Heater (+)       | Switched Power
Heater              | Negative (-)   | Power Bank GND   | Return Path
------------------------------------------------------------

[Part 3: Pin Reference for ESP32-S3]
------------------------------------------------------------
* RELAY_PIN: GPIO 2 (Marked as D2 on board)
* Status LED: Built-in (Shared with GPIO 2 on some models)
------------------------------------------------------------
